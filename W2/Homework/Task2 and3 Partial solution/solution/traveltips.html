  2. Check in at the airline counter
  3. Go through security
  4. Find the departure gate
  5. Buy a newspaper to read
  6. Board the plane
  7. Take off

First-Aid Supplies

Don't forget to pack these when adventuring:

  • Band-Aids
  • Bandage
  • Shark repellent
  • Aspirin
  • Snake-bite kit
  • Tweezers
  • Needle and thread

What's in a Bag?

Backpack
A bag for carrying hiking supplies and lunch
Fannypack
A bag for carrying a passport, wallet, and camera
Suitcase
A bag for carrying clothes and toiletries
Briefcase
A bag for carrying business documents, files, and pens

How to be Safe on your trip

  • Be aware of the main areas.
  • Ensure you have emergency contact numbers.
  • Keep a photocopy of your documents in a safe place.
  • Know what the sport is about.
  • Take your travel light.
  • Do your medical check-up before your trip

Be a Smart Traveller

  • Research your destination
  • Cover yourself with travel insurance
  • Organise your travel money
  • Vaccinations
  • Be conscious of your safety and security
  • Obey the law
